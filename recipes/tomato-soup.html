Tomato Soup

Description

Tomato soup is both easy-to-cook and delicious soup in many countries. Its main ingredient is tomato despite that it can be added for more refined tastes.

Ingredients

  • tomato
  • onion
  • garlic
  • celery
  • carrot
  • water

Steps

  1. Heat a pot with high heat
  2. Saute the onion, garlic, celery, and carrots
  3. Add tomatos of a can
  4. Add enough water and let it boil
  5. Finally, blend it
